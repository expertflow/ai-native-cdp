# Voice Setup Discussion 
**Context:** Kick-off of Voice Deployment Automation track (Tier 1 /track from session checkpoint)

---

## Approach

Rather than jumping to automation, the team agreed to start by surfacing the problems actually being encountered — regular and recent — with Voice setup and deployment in release testing.

---

## Problems Identified

1. **Back-and-forth among teams for campaign setup**  
   Deployment or configuration guide is not accessible to the QA/RMT team, or at minimum the RMT/QA team is unaware of how to do it. This creates friction and delays.

2. **No onboarding or overview of Voice components for RMT**  
   The RMT team has no walkthrough of how Voice components are deployed and configured. Every engagement requires tribal knowledge held by the team.
